HTML Links

HTML links, also known as hyperlinks, are an essential component of web design. They allow you to create clickable elements on your web pages that take your visitors to other pages or external resources. In this tutorial, we will cover everything you need to know about HTML links, including how to create them and how to customize their appearance.
